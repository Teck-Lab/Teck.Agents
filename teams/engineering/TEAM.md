# Engineering Team

Two full-stack dev teams, each with a Team Lead, Senior Developer, Junior Backend, and Junior Frontend developer. Database Engineer is shared across teams and reports to CTO.

## Structure
- **Dev Team 1**: Team Lead → Senior → Junior BE + Junior FE
- **Dev Team 2**: Team Lead → Senior → Junior BE + Junior FE

## Responsibilities
- Teck.Cloud: .NET 10 microservices, clean architecture, EF Core
- Teck.Web: Next.js 16, TypeScript, Turborepo, Tailwind CSS
- API contracts between backend and frontend
- Cross-service integration testing
