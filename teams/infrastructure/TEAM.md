# Infrastructure Team

Owns Teck.GitOps and Teck.Terraform — the deployment pipeline and platform tooling.

## Structure
- Team Lead → 2 Senior Infrastructure Engineers → 4 Junior Infrastructure Engineers

## Responsibilities
- ArgoCD app-of-apps, Kargo image promotion, Kustomize overlays
- OpenTofu Helm-based platform provisioning
- K3s cluster management
- CI/CD pipeline maintenance
- CNPG, Redis, RabbitMQ, Istio, cert-manager operations
