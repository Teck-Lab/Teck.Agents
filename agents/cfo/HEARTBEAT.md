# CFO HEARTBEAT — Run on scheduled cadence

## 1. Spend Review
- Model spend per squad: within budget?
- Any cost anomalies (unexpected spikes)?
- Fallback model chain usage: are expensive fallbacks firing frequently?

## 2. Budget Health
- Monthly burn rate vs. forecast: on track?
- Most expensive agents by spend: any optimization opportunities?
- Junior agents: are they staying on v4-flash?

## 3. ROI Analysis
- Features delivered this period vs. total spend
- Cost per task: trending up or down?
- Any low-ROI activities to cut?

## 4. Pricing Review
- Platform costs covered by pricing?
- Competitor pricing changes?
- Recommend pricing adjustments if needed

## 5. Extraction
- Document spend trends and anomalies
- Update budget forecasts
- Report to CEO on financial health
